Quemiloa S.A.

Our Company was born in 1989 in Argentina, with the name Impexsur. We are a family business, focused on the production, marketing, representation, export and sale of fish and other seafood, lagoons and rivers.

We have been growing, always maintaining our standards of quality, responsibility and strict compliance with our businesses.

Our goal is and will be to satisfy our customers.

The main offices are in Uruguay where we produce, represent, market and sell products from South America.

With us you will always have products with a high level of quality.

Let's keep growing together
